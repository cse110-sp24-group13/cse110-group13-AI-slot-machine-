# THE TOKEN BURNER: AI SYNERGY SLOT

A satirical slot machine built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## The Concept
"The Token Burner" makes fun of the current AI hype cycle. Players spend "Inference Tokens" (compute) to spin the reels in hopes of achieving "AGI" or "VC Funding." 

### Symbols & Payouts
- 🧠 **AGI**: The ultimate prize. 100x your bet.
- 💸 **VC_FUNDING**: Scaling for the sake of scaling. 50x your bet.
- 🤖 **LLM**: Just another model. 20x your bet.
- 🖥️ **GPU_CLUSTER**: High compute costs, low yield. 10x your bet.
- 🌫️ **HALLUCINATION**: The model made it up. If you get 3, you lose a massive amount of tokens.

## How to Play
1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Select your "Compute Intensity" (Bet size).
3. Click "SPIN (BURN TOKENS)" to start the inference process.
4. Watch the terminal logs for disruptive corporate synergy.

## Technical Details
- **Vanilla JS**: No frameworks.
- **CSS Grid/Flexbox**: Modern layout techniques.
- **CSS Transitions**: Smooth reel animations using `cubic-bezier`.
- **Cyber-Terminal Style**: Neon accents and CRT flicker effects.
